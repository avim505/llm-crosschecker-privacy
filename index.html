PRIVACY POLICY — LLM Cross-Checker Chrome Extension Last updated: May 2026 1. INTRODUCTION LLM Cross-Checker ("we", "our", or "the extension") is a Chrome browser extension that verifies AI responses across multiple AI models. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it. 2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT Anonymous Install ID We generate a random anonymous identifier when you install the extension. This is used solely to track your monthly usage count for the free tier limit. It contains no personal information. Usage Statistics We collect the number of cross-checks you perform each month and in total. This is used to enforce free tier limits and display your usage in the extension popup. Email Address If you subscribe to a paid plan, your email address is collected by ExtensionPay and Stripe for payment processing. We do not store your email address on our own servers. API Keys (BYOK users only) If you choose to use the Bring Your Own Key feature and voluntarily provide your own API keys, these are stored securely in our database and used only to make API calls on your behalf. You can delete them at any time. 3. AI RESPONSE TEXT When you activate Cross-Check, the text of the AI response is temporarily sent to our server and then to third-party AI providers for verification. This text is NOT stored and is discarded immediately after verification completes. 4. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES We share data with the following services: - Anthropic (Claude API) — AI verification - OpenAI (GPT API) — AI verification - Google (Gemini API) — AI verification - Cohere — AI verification - ExtensionPay — payment processing - Stripe — payment processing - MongoDB Atlas — database hosting - Render.com — server hosting Each service has its own privacy policy governing their use of data. 5. DATA WE DO NOT COLLECT We do not collect: - Your name or physical address - Your browsing history or pages visited - Your original prompts or questions to AI tools - Location data - Device information - Any data from websites other than ChatGPT and Claude 6. DATA RETENTION - Anonymous usage data is kept while your account is active - AI response text is never stored - Payment data is managed by ExtensionPay and Stripe - You can request deletion of all your data at any time 7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY This extension is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children. 8.
